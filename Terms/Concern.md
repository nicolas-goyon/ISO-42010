# Concern

A **Concern** is an interest in the [system](System.md) by one or more [stakeholders](Stakeholder.md).
A **Concern** pertains to any influence of a [system](System.md) in its [environment](Environment.md) :

- Developmental
- Technological
- Business
- Operational
- Organizational
- Political
- Economic
- Legal
- Regulatory
- Ecological
- Social
